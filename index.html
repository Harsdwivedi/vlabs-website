Introduction

Conducting experiments is frequently constrained by physical distances and the scarcity of resources, especially when complicated apparatus are used. To provide remote access to laboratories in several branches of research and engineering, virtual labs have been developed. These virtual labs serve undergraduate, graduate, post-doctoral, and research scholar students. The  Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, a project of the Indian government known as the Virtual Labs project, is under the auspices of the National Mission on Education through Information and Communication Technology (NMEICT), which serves as its umbrella body.Virtual Labs don't need any additional infrastructure settings to run tests on user premises. Online access is available for the simulation-based research from a distance. When remote-triggered studies were first conducted in 2010 with the Ministry's official support.
